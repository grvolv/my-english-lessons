  1. Sentence: The surging global popularity of TikTok has seen the co-founder of its parent company, ByteDance, become China's richest person.
    • Sentence Type: Complex
    • Conjunctions: None explicitly connecting clauses; implicit causality.
    • Main Clause: The surging global popularity of TikTok has seen…
      • Dependent Clause: …the co-founder of its parent company, ByteDance, become China’s richest person.
    • Grammatical Structure:
      • Subject: The surging global popularity of TikTok (Noun Phrase)
      • Predicate: has seen (Present Perfect, Active Voice)
      • Direct Object: the co-founder of its parent company, ByteDance, become China’s richest person.
    • Parts of Speech:
      • Verb: has seen (Present Perfect tense, indicating a completed action relevant to the present)
      • Nouns: popularity, TikTok, co-founder, company, ByteDance, person
      • Adjective: global (modifies popularity)
      • Infinitive Verb: become (used in the causative structure has seen [Object] become)
    • Analysis of Tense and Aspect: Present Perfect is used to show the recent or ongoing significance of TikTok's popularity impacting the status of its co-founder.
    • Syntactic Function: has seen functions as the main verb in a causative construction, where popularity is the subject causing the action of become.
    • Structural Analysis: The causative has seen is used with a base verb (become), marking a specific effect of TikTok’s popularity on the co-founder’s status.

      • Complement: $49.3bn (£38bn)

      • Adjective: rich (modifying list)
    • Analysis of Tense and Aspect: Present Simple is used to describe Zhang Yiming's current status.
    • Syntactic Function: is worth functions as a linking verb, attributing the value to the subject.
    • Structural Analysis: According to introduces a source, establishing reliability; is worth is a state construction commonly used for valuation.

      • Dependent Clause 2 (Condition Clause): unless ByteDance sells it
    • Grammatical Structure:
      • Subject: the US
      • Predicate: intends to ban (Present Simple, Active Voice)
      • Direct Object: TikTok
      • Adverbial Clause of Condition: unless ByteDance sells it
    • Parts of Speech:
      • Verb: intends (Present Simple, Active, showing planned action); sells (Simple Present in a conditional clause)
      • Nouns: companies, government, US, ByteDance
      • Adjective: independent (describes they)
    • Analysis of Tense and Aspect: Present Simple for future intentions (intends) and hypothetical condition (unless sells).
    • Syntactic Function: Main clause describes future action dependent on a condition; unless introduces a limiting condition.
    • Structural Analysis: The contrast is established with while, emphasizing the claimed independence against possible government influence.
